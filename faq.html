Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is included in your interior detailing service?
Our interior detailing includes deep vacuuming of carpets and seats, full shampooing of carpets and upholstery, leather cleaning and conditioning, dashboard and console cleaning, and odour removal. We aim to leave your interior spotless and fresh.
2. What does the exterior detailing service involve?
Exterior detailing includes hand washing, clay bar treatment to remove contaminants, machine polishing, tire and rim cleaning, and protective wax or sealant application to restore gloss and protection to your paintwork.
3. How long does a full detail take?
Depending on the package and vehicle size, interior or exterior details typically take 2-4 hours. Full interior & exterior detailing may take 4-6 hours. We recommend booking ahead to secure your preferred time.
4. Do I need to book in advance?
Yes, booking in advance ensures we can schedule your vehicle at a convenient time and avoid delays. Walk-ins are occasionally accepted but are not guaranteed.
5. Can you come to my location?
Yes, we offer mobile detailing within the Manawatu area. Please provide your location during booking, and we‚Äôll confirm availability.
6. Are your products safe for my car?
Absolutely. We use professional-grade detailing products that are safe for all car surfaces, including paint, glass, leather, and plastics.
7. How much does a full detail cost?
Our prices vary depending on the package and vehicle size. Popular packages start from $139 for interior, $179 for exterior, and $499 for ceramic coating. Contact us for a custom quote.
8. Can you remove stubborn stains or odours?
Yes, we specialize in stain removal and odour elimination. Results depend on the severity and age of stains, but we always aim for the best outcome.
9. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfer, and most major debit/credit cards. Payment is typically made at the time of service.
10. Do you offer any warranties or guarantees?
Yes, we guarantee our work. If you are not satisfied with your detailing service, please contact us within 48 hours so we can address any issues.
